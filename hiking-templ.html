Top 3 Reccomended Hiking Trails

These are my top 3 reccomended hiking trails at this current time. This small list offers a variety in terms of length and scenery.

# Trail Name Trail Length Trail Description
1 McCormicks Creek State Park Falls Canyon Trail 4.9 miles 1.5 to 2 hours to complete Be prepared to get wet on this hike. This tails leads to a beautiful natural waterfall that offers swimming. Gate admission is required due to being in a state park.
2 Yellowwood Lake Trail in Yellowwood state forest 4.5 miles 1.5 to 2 hours This trail offers a beautiful lake view and wildlife. No fee required.
3 Turkey Run State Park, trails 3,10,9,5 5.5 miles 2.5 hours Another beautiful state park trail system. These trails offer a variety along with water, ladders and mud (pack your boots!). These trails do require an adimission fee.
