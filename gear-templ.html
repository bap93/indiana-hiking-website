Gear

Backpacking and hiking gear can be very expensive and a trial and error process. Below are must hav items for hiking and backpacking. Many of these items can be found at REI. I highly suggest becoming a member if you plan on purchasing a lot, as the membership is very afforable and a one time fee (with the best return policy!!). Also, don't be afraid to buy second hand gear, REI has second hand gear that is much more afforable than new. Ebay is also a great option. I have found many quality items at thrift shops acorss the states as well.

  • Day pack
  • Backpacking pack
  • Quality water reservoir
  • Headlamp
  • Knife
  • Camp stove with fuel
  • Water filter
  • Light weight cooking pot and a spork
  • Light weight sleeping pad
  • Light weight sleeping bag
  • Light weight tent
  • Quick dry clothes and smartwool socks
  • Quality boots or hiking shoes
  • Lighter
  • Matches
  • Whistle
  • Dry bag
  • First aid kit
  • Trowel

This list is just he basics and may vary from what you will come to prefer. Of course, do not forget to pack meals and snacks along with things like: Batteries, personal products, and camp sandals.
