Top 2 Recommended backpacking Trails for beginners

These are my top 2 recommended backpacking trails for beginners at this current time. These two trails are in the Charles Deam Wilderness located in the Hoosier National Forest. For more information on trails, visit the resources page for reccomended trail applications.

Check out the resources page for important information about backpacking regulations.

# Trail Name Trail Length Trail Description
1 Sycamore Loop Trail 6.40 miles 508ft elevation gain This trail has camping in marked areas. Be mindful of the signs that dictate areas that camping is prohibited. This trail offers less elevation and is a short loop, perfect for beginners.
2 Lake Monroe Peninsula Trail 10.10 miles 711ft elevation gain This trail is an out and back that leads you to the shores of lake monroe.
